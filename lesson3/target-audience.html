Target Audience

The visiors of this website want to be informed about the local weather in their area. They are paricularly interested in tracking servere weather.

Persona 1: Paula

Paula is 38 years old and married with 3 children in the age of 14, 11, and 9 years old. She runs her own business as wedding planner and works together with other small businesses like catering services or video- and photographers.

Persona 2: Robert

Robert is 28 years old, single and works as stocker in a local supermarket. In his freetime, he is an avid hunter. For this purpose he owns two trained and certified hunting dogs.

Scenario 1

Many of the weddings Paula is planning take place outside. This make it neccecery for her to be aware of the weather so she can make alternative plans if servere weather threatens.

Scenario 2

Paula's 14 year old son need to track the weather for a school project. This works excellent with this website as we offer a 7 day forecast

Scenario 3

Paula's husband is also interested in the weather as his hobby is photography. The weather plays an important role in planning a photo shoot.

Scenario 4

For Robert it is cruicial to know about the weather during the hunting season so he knows what gear he has to pack.

Scenario 5

Also outside the hunting season Robert depends on the weather. His dogs train best in fair weather conditions.
