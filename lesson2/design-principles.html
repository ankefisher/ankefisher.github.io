Repetition

The colors and motifs of the dispayed picture are repeated in the other one. This is the business page of an art gallery and the customer can immediatly see what art work belongs together.

Alignment

www.wendemuseum.org

This is not the best example of a mobile web page but I like the alignment of the page elements. They all have the same height, width and baseline.

White Space

ao5gallery.com

This page uses White Space to separate the additional information and the logo from the content of the page. It gives the page a user friendly structure.
